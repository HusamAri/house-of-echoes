The Approach

Four movements,
one echo.

  1. 01

    Listen

    We start in silence, listening for the brand, the room, the feeling you want to leave behind.

  2. 02

    Frame

    Treatment, shot list and light. Every choice is intentional before a camera is ever switched on.

  3. 03

    Capture

    A lean, precise crew on set. Cinema cameras, considered light, golden hours chased and held.

  4. 04

    Echo

    Edit, color and sound, finished to linger. Delivered for screen, cinema and every feed.

Studio

Hakan Güneş

Founder · Director & Cinematographer

House of Echoes is the studio of Hakan Güneş, a director who treats light as a language and restraint as a style. For a decade he has chased the hour between day and night, building films for restaurants, hotels and brands that don't shout to be remembered.

The studio works as a full-round partner: strategy, direction, cinematography and post under one roof. Based in Istanbul, filming worldwide, wherever there is a story worth letting echo.
